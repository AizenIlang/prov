ABOUT

US

Hello! , We are the LGNDS bonafide Fourth Year students in Bachelor of Science in Information Technology from Jose Rizal University who were currently enrolled in IT Project 2.

The major requirement of this course is to come up with a system that can possibly address any problem that is currently being experienced by society. We, the Team LGNDS came up with a system proposal entitled “PROV: H – Locator: A Hospital Finder for Specific Needs and Services”. It is our main goal in this study that we will be able to generate a system that can help the entire people of Metro Manila in finding the best hospital for their specific needs.

With this, we are respectfully introducing to you the “PROV: H – Locator”. The best hospital finder that capable on helping you and your future needs. “The power of community to create health is far greater than any physician, clinic or hospital.” – Mark Hyman
